The Problem

Your company spends ₹40–60L a year on employee transport — and can't account for any of it.

Indian enterprises manage transport as a black box. The result: wasted spend, failed ESG audits, and 1,200 frustrated commutes every day.

ESG Compliance Gap

Scope 3 employee commute emissions are mandatory for BRSR compliance but impossible to report without transport data infrastructure. Most companies are flying blind on their audit.

Scope 3 untracked
The Solution

FlowPath OS: one AI layer that solves all three.

  • Multimodal AI Routing Engine

    Learns each employee's commute DNA — price sensitivity, walking tolerance, departure flexibility — and dispatches optimal routes across metro, bus, auto, and cab in real time.

  • Verified Reimbursement Layer

    Every trip is logged, time-stamped, and cross-referenced with actuals. Finance gets a clean, auditable transport ledger. No more unverifiable claims.

  • Automated Scope 3 ESG Reports

    Auto-generates BRSR-compliant Scope 3 commute emissions reports from live trip data. One click from audit-ready PDF.

  • Crowdsourced Transit Intelligence

    Employees report real-time delays (Waze-style) — bootstrapping a proprietary transit graph that improves with every commuter on the platform.

  • HRMS Integration

    Connects with SAP SuccessFactors, Darwinbox, and Workday. Transport data flows directly into payroll and expense management systems.
